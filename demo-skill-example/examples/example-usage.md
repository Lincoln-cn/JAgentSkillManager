# Usage Examples

## Basic Calculation

**User:** "What's 15 plus 27?"

**Parameters:**
- operation: "add"
- a: 15
- b: 27

**Result:** 42

## Complex Operation

**User:** "Calculate the product of 8 and 9"

**Parameters:**
- operation: "multiply"
- a: 8
- b: 9

**Result:** 72

## Division

**User:** "Divide 100 by 4"

**Parameters:**
- operation: "divide"
- a: 100
- b: 4

**Result:** 25

## Error Handling

**User:** "Divide by zero"

**Parameters:**
- operation: "divide"
- a: 10
- b: 0

**Result:** Error - Division by zero is not allowed
